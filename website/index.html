How it works

Three steps to zero confusion

Upload your process once. Every new hire gets a personal AI guide from day one.

Step 01
📄
Upload your SOP

Drop in a PDF, Word doc, or plain text. HandOff AI reads it and generates step-by-step instructions automatically in seconds.

Step 02
🔗
Share a link

Copy the onboarding link from your admin dashboard and send it to your new hire. One click and they're in — no login, no setup.

🤖

The HandOff widget floats above the screen, reads each step aloud, watches the screen, and guides the user to the right place.
