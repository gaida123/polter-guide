How it works

Running in 3 steps

Upload your process once. Every new hire gets a personal AI guide from day one.

Step 01
Upload your SOP

Drop in a PDF, Word doc, or plain text description. HandOff AI reads it and generates step-by-step instructions in seconds using Gemini.

Step 02
Share a link

Copy the onboarding link from your admin dashboard and send it to your new hire. One click — no login, no app download required.

Step 03
AI guides in real time

The HandOff widget floats above every window, reads each step aloud, watches the screen, and helps when the user gets stuck.

Infrastructure

Powered by autonomous
AI agents

HandOff deploys a network of Fetch.ai uAgents that handle vision, context, knowledge retrieval, and post-session reporting — continuously, without human intervention.

Fetch.ai uAgents — always running

Your onboarding, on autopilot

Four specialized agents work in the background so every session is smooth, verified, and tracked — automatically.

ContextAgent
Orchestrates vision and knowledge — decides what guidance to surface next based on the current screen
VisionAgent
Analyses screenshots to verify the employee is on the correct screen before each step advances
KnowledgeAgent
Retrieves the right SOP steps and serves them to the widget in real-time as the user progresses
CompletionAgent
Scans for completed sessions every 10 seconds, generates AI summaries, and feeds them to the admin dashboard automatically
